About


As with most small towns, our population isn't vast, but surprisingly, there is a lot of diversity in age groups.. Families, small or big, seniors and kids, all have a place in this town. The common interests of the town are all the clubs that tie the community together. The people are amiable, chatty, and overall pleasant. Within this community, no matter if you were born and raised here or just passing by, if you are in trouble, you'll never struggle to find help. There's a helping hand waiting for you. Most of the population speaks English, with a few speaking French. One day, we hope to add more diversity to the culture by welcoming new visitors who turn into residents. It's a small countryside town filled with lovely people.
